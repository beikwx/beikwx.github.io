## Rule 1.Primary amine 1

> 1.Primary amines have NH2 groups. Name is written by replacing last 'e' of parent by 'amine'。

1.乙基胺有NH2基团，书写时将母链结尾'e'替换为'amine'。

![一级胺][01]

> 2.Numbering is done so that NH2 gets lower number.

2.编号让NH2位数低。

![编号][02]

> 3.locant of amine group is written before 'amine'。

3.氨基的位置写在'amine'前面。

![位置][03]

![环胺][12]

1.当氨基在苯环上就叫苯胺。

## Rule 4.Secondary amine

> 1.In secondary amines NH group is present. Alkyl group with longest possible carbon chain is choosen as parent. Number the chain so that 'NH' gets minimum number. Ohter alkyl group is regared as substituent attached to 'N'。

1.在二级胺中，NH作为基团。选择最长的烷基作为母链，编号让NH位置最低。其他的烷基作为N的取代基。

![二级胺][33]

> 2.If an alkyl group is present on 'N' and at another locantion on parent chain, it's name can be written as(N,locants-name of alkyl)

2.如果取代基和N相连，并且在母链上有其他位置也有这个取代基，写作(N,烷烃位置)见例子。

![N取代][34]

> 3.In case of benzene or cycloalkanes attached to NH, they are taken as parent.

3.如果苯或者环烷烃上有二级胺，他们作为母链。

![环二级胺][35]

## Rule 5.Tertiary amine

> 1.In tertiary amines N group is present. Alkyl group with longest possible carbon chain is choosen as parent. Number the chain so that 'N' gets minimum number. Other alkyl groups is regared as substituent attached to 'N'.

1.三级胺中N作为基团。最长链的烷基作为母链，编号让N最低，其他的基团作为N的取代基。

![三级胺][44]

> 2.If an alkyl group is present on 'N' and at another location on parent chain, it's name can be written as (N,locants-name of alkyl)

2.母链上的取代基在N上也有，同二级胺。

![多取代三级胺][45]

> 3.In case of benzene or cycloalkanes attached to N, they taken as parent.

3.环烷烃和苯和N相连时，他们作为母链。

![环烷烃][46]
