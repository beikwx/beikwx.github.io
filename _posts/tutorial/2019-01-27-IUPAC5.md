## Rule 1.Straight chain Aldehydes(直链醛)

> 1.Select longest chain starting from CHO, number the chain with 1 to C of CHO. Write parent alkane as alkanal by replacing 'e' by 'al'

1.从CHO开始选择最长链，CHO的碳编号为1.书写时母烷烃将'e'改为'al'

![醛][01]

> 2.Write substituents in alphabetical order.

2.按照字母顺序书写取代基

![取代][02]

> 3.If double or triple bond are present, start numbering from CHO, and write name of parent by replacing last 'e' of alkene or alkyne by 'al'

3.如果醛还有双键和三键，书写时将烯烃或者炔烃的'e'改为'al'

![双键三键][03]

## Rule 2.Cyclic aldehydes(环醛)

> 1.If CHO is present on ring, write name of parent as cycloalkane(retaing last 'e') followed by carbaldehyde.

1.如果环上有醛基，环烷烃作为母链（保留最后的'e'）接着写甲醛。

![环醛][14]

> 2.If other substituents are present on ring besides CHO, numbering the ring giving corner attached to CHO number '1' and then number in clockwise or anticlockwise manner so that other substituents gets lower number.

2.如果环上面有取代，醛基编号1，之后取代基规则编号。

![取代环醛][15]

> 3.CHO attached to benzene is called benzaldehyde. If other substituents are present, number the ring, giving CHO '1'

3.如果醛基在苯上，叫做苯甲醛。如果还有其他取代，让醛基位置为'1'。

![苯甲醛][16]

## Rule 3.Poly aldehydes

> 1.If two CHO are present and they belong to same chain, number the chain from that end, from where other substituents are nearer. Write name by adding 'dial' to name of parent alkane.

1.如果同一条链上有2个醛，从一个醛编号到另一个醛并且让取代基更小。结尾加入'dial'。

![多醛][27]

> 2.If two or more CHO are present on ring, number the ring so that all CHO gets minimum number and write name of ring(retaining last 'e') followed by carbaldehyde adding prefix like di, tri etc.

2.如果环上有2个或者更多的醛，标号让所有的醛最小（保留最后的'e')接着用在甲醛前面加入2，3之类的前缀。

![多取代环][28]  
（注意不叫苯甲醛了）

> 3.If three or more CHO are present on chain, select the longest chain(retaining last 'e') followed by carbaldehyde adding prefix like di, tri, etc.

3.如果链上有更多醛，选择最长链（保留结尾e）接着在甲醛前面加上2，3之类的前缀。（见练习）

![多取代][29]

## Straight chain Ketones(直链酮)

> 1.Select longest chain in which CO group is present and number the chain so that CO gets minimum number. Write name by replacing last 'e' of parent by 'one' adding locant of CO as prefix.

1.选择包含CO基团的最长链，并且CO最低。书写时将结尾的‘e'改为'one'并在前面加上CO的位置作为前缀。

![酮的命名][40]

> 2.If other substituents are present, write their name in alphabetically order.

2.如果有其他取代基，按照字母顺序书写。

![取代酮][41]

> 3.If double or triple bond are present, number the chain to give CO minimum number.

3.如果有双键和三键，让CO的位置最低。

![烯炔酮][42]

## Rule 5.cyclic ketones

> 1.If CO is presnt as part of ring, write name by replacing last 'e' of parent by 'one'.

1.如果酮在环烷烃上，书写时将环烷烃结尾'e'改为'one'。

![环酮][53]

> 2.If other substituents are present besides CO, number the ring, giving CO attached corner '1'.

2.如果环烷烃上除了酮还有别的取代，CO的地方编号为1.

![取代环酮][54]

> 3.If two sets of locants are possible, choose one in which alphabetically priority group gets minimum number.

3.如果有多个相同位置，让字母顺序靠前的位置最小。

![相同位置][55]
